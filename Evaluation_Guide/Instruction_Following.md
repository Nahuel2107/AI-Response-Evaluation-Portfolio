# Instruction Following

## Definition

Instruction Following measures whether the AI correctly follows all parts of the user's request.

## What to Look For

- Answers every part of the prompt.
- Does not ignore important instructions.
- Uses the requested format.
- Stays on topic.

## Example

Prompt:

List three fruits.

Correct Answer:

- Apple
- Banana
- Orange

Incorrect Answer:

Apple

## Why It Matters

Even accurate information is not useful if the AI fails to follow the user's instructions.
