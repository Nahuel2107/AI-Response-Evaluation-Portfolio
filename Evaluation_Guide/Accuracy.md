# Accuracy

## Definition

Accuracy measures whether the information provided by the AI is factually correct and free from errors.

## What to Look For

- Correct facts.
- No misinformation.
- No invented information.
- Consistency with reliable knowledge.

## Example

Question:
What is the capital of France?

Correct Answer:
Paris.

Incorrect Answer:
Lyon.

## Why It Matters

Accurate responses build trust and ensure the AI provides reliable information.
